About Us

H&J FIBRE INVESTMENT CO. LTD About Us
Who we are

H&J FIBRE INVESTMENT CO. LTD

H&J Fibre Investment (T) Co. Ltd is a trusted manufacturer and supplier of high-quality Polyester Staple Fibre (PSF) and related synthetic fibres. We serve the textile, nonwoven, automotive, home furnishing, filtration, and construction industries with reliable, performance-driven fibre solutions.

Our Mission

To explore and produce packaging materials for sustainable development at highest standards of industrial practices, social responsibilities and environment sustainability by optimizing our productive resources.

Our Vision

To become one of the most relevant companies in Tanzania, creating sustainable value to shareholders, employees, suppliers, customers, business partners and host communities through production and marketing of packaging materials.

Goals

Our main Objectives

With modern production facilities and strict quality control systems, we deliver consistent products that meet international standards while supporting sustainable manufacturing practices.

  • To promote the local market for PP woven bags in Tanzania.
  • To increase the quality of PP woven bags manufactured in Tanzania.
  • To reduce dependence on external sources for the importation of PP woven bags.
  • To encourage the growth of the PP woven bag industry in Tanzania.
  • To create employment for the local people
  • To contribute to the countrys economy through taxes and other levies.
  • To promote growth and expansion of the private sector.
  • To boost the export market in SADC and COMESA countries, resulting in increased foreign earnings.
