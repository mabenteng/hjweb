About us

H&J FIBRE INVESTMENT CO. LTD

H&J Fibre Investment (T) Co. Ltd is a leading manufacturing company established in November 2017 and registered under the Export Processing Zones Authority (EPZA), now Tanzania Investment and Special Economic Zones Authority (TISEZA). Our operations are based in Pwani Region, Bagamoyo District, Pingo Chalinze, adjacent to KEDA Ceramic Factory, Tanzania.

Chemical Fiber Products and PP Woven Bags

We specialize in the production of chemical fiber products and PP woven bags, supported by a fully integrated industrial production chain. Our advanced spinning and processing facilities enable a complete, end-to-end manufacturing process from recycling waste plastic bottles to producing high-quality chemical fiber and finished woven products.

Advanced Recycling Solutions

H&J Fibre Investment (T) Co. Ltd is committed to operational excellence, sustainability, and innovation, delivering consistent quality and value to our customers while contributing to environmental responsibility through advanced recycling solutions.
